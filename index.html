Family CTO

A title for the job you have been doing for years,
and a community to support you in the years to come.

So, you're a Family CTO?

If you have been supporting the technical infrastructure for friends and loved ones, this is a movement for you. Let's stop figuring all this stuff out ourselves. Let's get some simple checklists together. Let's share our insights, successes and failures.

You drive the tech adoption curve

Because you have taken on the thankless task of keeping everyone safe, productive and happy, you also drive adoption of technology products, services and practices.

Just getting started here

There are a million things to do here - but let's start with some of the basics. Should every family have there own domain (or three)? How do you keep track of all the devices folks are using?
